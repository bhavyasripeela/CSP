Health Awareness

Seasonal tips, safety & hygiene

Seasonal Alert
☀️
Heatwave Safety — Summer 2025
Hyderabad is experiencing extreme heat. Protect yourself:
  • Drink at least 3–4 litres of water daily
  • Avoid going out between 12 PM – 4 PM
  • Wear light, loose, light-coloured clothing
  • If dizzy or nauseous, sit in shade and drink ORS
  • Never leave children or elderly in parked vehicles
Hydration Tips
💧
Stay Hydrated
Signs of dehydration to watch for:
  • Dark yellow urine — drink more water immediately
  • Headache & dizziness — sign of early dehydration
  • ORS sachets available free at hospital pharmacy
  • Avoid sugary drinks & alcohol in heat
Hygiene & Prevention
🧼
Hygiene Practices
Simple habits that prevent most diseases:
  • Wash hands before eating and after toilet
  • Drink only boiled or filtered water
  • Cover mouth while coughing or sneezing
  • Do not self-medicate — consult a doctor
